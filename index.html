Projects

Plant Flooding

Worked with professor Richard Barker who is studying plant growth in space. Our group devloped and coded an arduino to detect water level using a float switch. When water level is too low, water is automatically pumped into the plant tray in order to keep plant roots flooded; This simulates a low gravity environment. Our prototype is pictured above.

Speedwagon Project

Dubbed the "Speedwagon Project" because an image I saw online of a character named "Speedwagon" that was created with smaller versions of itself served as inspiration for this small, silly project. Used python to program a simple image processor that recreates an image using smaller versions of itself.
